## ⚡ Quick Actions

### Tambah Produk Baru
```
1. Products & Recipes → Add Product
2. Isi: Name, Category, SKU, Price
3. Add Ingredient → Pilih ingredient + quantity
4. Review cost calculation
5. Save Product
```

### Update Harga Ingredient
```
1. Ingredients & Pricing → Cari ingredient
2. Click Edit (✏️)
3. Ubah Cost Per Unit
4. Click Save (💾)
5. ✅ All product costs auto-recalculated!
```

### Edit Recipe Produk
```
1. Products & Recipes → Click Edit on product
2. Modify recipe (add/remove/change quantity)
3. Review updated cost
4. Save Product
```

## 💡 Tips & Tricks

### Profit Margin Target:
- ✅ **Good**: 50-60%
- ⚠️ **Warning**: 30-40%
- ❌ **Low**: < 30%

### Stock Status:
- 🟢 **Good**: Stock > 150% min stock
- 🟡 **Warning**: Stock 100-150% min stock
- 🔴 **Low**: Stock ≤ min stock

### Price Update Frequency:
- **Weekly**: Fast-moving ingredients (susu, kopi)
- **Monthly**: Slow-moving ingredients (gula, cup)
- **As needed**: Seasonal items

## 🆘 Common Issues

### "Cost tidak update setelah ubah harga ingredient"
**Fix:** Refresh halaman Products & Recipes

### "Profit margin negatif"
**Fix:** 
1. Review recipe quantity
2. Check ingredient prices
3. Adjust selling price

### "Ingredient tidak muncul di dropdown"
**Fix:** Tambahkan ingredient dulu via Inventory page
